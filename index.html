Buzzer for the Blind

Our Mission

Our product, the Buzzer for the Blind, is made to simplify and make the process of learning the layout of a new space or house way easier for any blind person. There are many challenges in becoming blind and having to learn to lavigate the world by touch and memory, and this product is made to aliviate most, if not all, or the stress of doing so.
